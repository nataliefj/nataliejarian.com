A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Hamilton, Canada, though I grew up in Australia. My journey into tech started recently, when I decided to take my career into my own hands. I have previously worked in administration and customer service - both in the construction industry. I've always been the 'techy friend' and so decided to grow in that area, into an industry that is here to stay.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
